Fonts

Roboto: I choose this font for headers because it looks sleek and is refreshing, which can easily stand out as a header/title.

Open Sans: I choose this font for paragraphs because it is easy to read and scan through, keeping in mind my target audience would be future employers, who need to scan and judge my work quickly.
