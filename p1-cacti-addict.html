Logo Design for Cacti Addict - Startup Business

Freelance // Australia // 2018

Adobe Illustrator

Objective

This project was an opportunity to push me in designing a logo around the ideas related to terrarium and start-up business.

Figure 1: Final Product

Ideation Stage

I gathered inspiration from Pinterest and Google and made a quick mood board to illustrate the visual concept. I used to think mood board was a waste of time during my academic projects, but trying this with a real-world client I realized it gave us both an easier time in imagining and communicating the ideas.

Figure 2: Moodboard with a blend of blue, green, and pink color and realistically complex style. All images taken from Pinterest.

Figure 3: Sketches for the logo.

Figure 4: Refined the sketches to low-medium fidelity for easier visualization.

Design Problem

The color was too bright and the overall look lacked a focal point.

Refinement of Design

I changed the color to a darker blue-greenish with a touch of pink. The outline color for the case is also darker to create a stronger focus on the inner object.

Figure 5: Integrated the client's feedbacks with the original inspiration for the final outcome.
